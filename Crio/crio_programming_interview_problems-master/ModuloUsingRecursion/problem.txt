You are given two numbers a and b. You have to find the remainder when a is divided by b.
If remainder is not possible then return -1.

Constraints:
0<= a,b <=1000000


Input:
You will be given two space separated integers a and b.

Output:
The remainder of a when divided by b.If remainder is not possible then print -1.

Example:
=======

Input:

10 3

Output:

1
