Given n non-negative integers representing the histogram's bar height
where the width of each bar is 1, find the area of largest rectangle in the histogram.

=========
Example 1
=========

Histogram:

      *
    * *
    * *
    * *   *
*   * * * *
* * * * * *
---------------------------------

Input
6
2 1 5 6 2 3

Output
10

=========
Example 2
=========

Histogram:


*   *
* * *
----------------------------------

Input
3
2 1 2

Output
3
