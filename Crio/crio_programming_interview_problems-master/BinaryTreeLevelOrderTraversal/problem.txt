Given a binary tree, return the level order traversal of its nodes' values.(ie, from left to right, level by level).


=========
Example 1
=========
Input
4
4 3 2 1

Treeview
     4
  3      2
1   x  x   x

Output
4
3 2
1

=========
Example 2
=========
Input
7
-5 5 2 null 4 null 5

Treeview
     -5
  5       2
x   4   x   5

Output
-5
5 2
4 5
